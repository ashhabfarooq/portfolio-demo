  • Tools: Arduino, Python

Real World Design Project — Assistive Dog Leash System

Design

Developed a forearm-supported dog leash concept designed primarily for elderly users with weakened grip strength. The system integrates a seatbelt-inspired locking mechanism to reduce accident risk during sudden pulling forces, while the forearm support distributes load more comfortably and minimizes strain on the hand and wrist.

  • Focus: ergonomic design, user safety, accessibility
  • Approach: iterative prototyping, real-world usability
